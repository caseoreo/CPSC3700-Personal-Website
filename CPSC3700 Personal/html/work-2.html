    Hiroshige Whirlpool Remix

    Medium: Print
    Original Dimensions: 17 in x 24 in
    Created: 2022

    This print was made using Adobe Illustrator. The goal was to take a pre-existing artwork and remix it in a way that would relate to a topic today. Through this, I remixed Hiroshige's "Naruto Whirlpool" ca. 1853 to emphasize the importance of water by putting his artwork within a tea cup.
